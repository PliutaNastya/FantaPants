How to Purchase FantaPants Tokens:

  1. Step 1: Download a SUI Wallet

    To get started, you’ll need to download a wallet that supports the SUI network.

    • Chrome Extension: Sui Wallet Chrome Extension
    • Android: Sui Wallet on Google Play
    • iOS: Sui wallet on the App Store

    After installing Sui, follow the instructions to set up your wallet.

  2. Step 2: Set Up Your SUI Wallet

    Once you’ve downloaded the SUI wallet, follow these steps to set it up:

    • Open the Wallet: Launch the wallet app/extension.
    • Create a New Wallet: Choose the option to create a new wallet.
    • Backup Your Seed Phrase: Make sure to securely store your seed phrase. It’s essential to keep it safe as it’s your only way to recover your wallet.
    • Set Your Password: Set a strong password to secure your wallet.
  3. Step 3: Fund Your SUI Wallet

    To interact with the SUI network, you need to fund your wallet with SUI tokens for transaction fees.

    Get SUI Tokens:

    You can acquire SUI tokens in a few different ways:

    • Buy on a Centralized Exchange:

      You can purchase SUI tokens on exchanges like Coinspot, Binance, or KuCoin.

    • Direct Transfer:

      If someone is sending you SUI tokens, simply provide them with your Sui wallet address.

    Send SUI to Your SUI Wallet:

    • Find Your Wallet Address:
      • Open your SUI wallet app, click on your wallet address, and copy it.
    • Send SUI from Exchange:
      • On Coinspot (or any other exchange where you bought SUI), go to "Send" and paste your SUI wallet address.
      • Choose the amount of SUI tokens you want to send, then confirm the transaction.
    • Wait for the Transfer:
      • After a few minutes, the SUI tokens will appear in your SUI wallet, and you’re ready to make your purchase.
  4. Step 4: Connect Your Wallet to Turbos Finance

    Now that your wallet is funded with SUI tokens, it’s time to purchase FantaPants.

    • Go to Turbos Finance:
      • Visit Turbos Finance in your browser.
    • Connect Your Wallet:
      • On the top right of the website, click "Connect Wallet" and select SUI wallet.
      • Follow the prompts to connect your SUI wallet to Turbos Finance.
  5. Step 5: Swap SUI for FantaPants Tokens:
    • Select the Amount:
      • Enter the number of SUI tokens you want to swap for FantaPants.
    • Click "Trade":
      • Once you’ve entered the amount, click “Trade” to initiate the transaction.
    • Confirm the Transaction:
      • Your SUI wallet will prompt you to confirm the transaction. Review the details and confirm it.
  7. Step 6: View Your FantaPants Tokens

    Once the transaction is complete:

    • Check Your Wallet:
      • Open your SUI wallet, and you’ll see your newly purchased FantaPants tokens.
